How Emsella Works

BTL Emsella uses High-Intensity Focused Electromagnetic (HIFEM) technology to stimulate deep pelvic floor muscles that you can't effectively target with traditional exercises.

1

Sit Comfortably

You remain fully clothed and sit comfortably on the Emsella chair.

2

Electromagnetic Stimulation

The chair delivers thousands of supramaximal pelvic floor muscle contractions.

3

Muscle Rebuilding

These contractions strengthen and rebuild your pelvic floor muscles, restoring support and function.

4

See Results

Most patients notice improvement after just 2-3 sessions, with optimal results after the full treatment series.

Common Questions

Does it hurt?

Not at all! Most patients describe a tingling sensation and muscle contractions that feel like an intense workout, but it's not painful. You can adjust the intensity for comfort.

Do I need to undress?

No! You remain fully clothed throughout the entire treatment. Simply wear comfortable clothing.

Who is a good candidate?

Ideal for anyone experiencing stress incontinence, urge incontinence, postpartum pelvic floor weakness, or age-related decline in pelvic muscle function.

Are there any side effects?

CoreLift with Emsella has no downtime and minimal side effects. Some patients experience temporary muscle soreness similar to a workout.

How long do results last?

With the complete treatment series, most patients enjoy lasting results for 1-2 years. Periodic maintenance sessions can extend benefits indefinitely.
