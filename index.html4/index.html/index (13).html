CoreLift Pelvic Health with Emsella

Revolutionary Non-Invasive Treatment

CoreLift uses FDA-cleared Emsella technology to strengthen pelvic floor muscles through high-intensity electromagnetic energy. This groundbreaking treatment addresses urinary incontinence, improves intimate health, and restores confidence—all without surgery or downtime.

What to Expect:

  • Remain fully clothed during treatment
  • Sit comfortably on the Emsella chair
  • 28-minute sessions feel like gentle tingling
  • Series of 6 treatments over 3 weeks recommended
  • No preparation or recovery time needed

Benefits:

  • 95% of patients report improved quality of life
  • Strengthens pelvic floor muscles
  • Reduces urinary incontinence episodes
  • Improves intimate health
  • No surgery, no downtime
  • Results typically noticed after 2-3 sessions

About Dr. Tess Garcia

Compassionate, Faith-Based Family Medicine

📸
Photo: Dr. Tess Garcia

Dr. Tess Garcia founded Garcia Family Medicine with a simple mission: to provide personalized, compassionate healthcare that treats the whole patient—Spirit, Body, and Soul.

Through our Direct Primary Care model, we've removed the barriers that traditional insurance creates between doctors and patients. This means longer appointments, same-day or next-day visits, and 24/7 access to your doctor via text, email, or phone.

At Garcia Family Medicine, we believe healthcare should be a partnership. We take the time to listen, understand your unique needs, and create personalized treatment plans that honor your values and beliefs.
