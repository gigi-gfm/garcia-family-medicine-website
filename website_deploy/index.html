Program Includes:

  • Initial consultation and health assessment
  • GLP-1 medication management (Semaglutide or Tirzepatide)
  • Monthly follow-up appointments
  • Personalized meal planning and nutrition coaching
  • Exercise recommendations tailored to you
  • Ongoing support and accountability
  • Lab monitoring as needed
  • Lifestyle modification strategies

How GLP-1 Works:

GLP-1 medications work with your body's natural hormones to reduce appetite, slow digestion, and help you feel full longer. Combined with lifestyle changes, patients typically lose 15-20% of their body weight.

Perfect for: Anyone struggling with weight loss despite diet and exercise, those with obesity-related health conditions, or anyone seeking a medically supervised weight loss program.

Dr. Tess Garcia is a board-certified family medicine physician dedicated to providing personalized, compassionate care to patients in Blue Springs and the greater Kansas City metro area.
