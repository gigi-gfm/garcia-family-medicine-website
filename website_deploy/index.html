Dr. Garcia provides comprehensive evaluations with detailed written reports. What is an Independent Medical Evaluation (IME)? An Independent Medical Evaluation is a comprehensive medical examination performed by an independent physician to provide an objective assessment of an individual's medical condition, disability status, or treatment needs. IMEs are often requested by insurance companies, employers, attorneys, or courts to obtain an unbiased medical opinion. Common Reasons for IMEs: Workers' Compensation Claims: Evaluation of work-related injuries or illnesses Disability Determinations: Assessment for short-term or long-term disability benefits Personal Injury Cases: Medical evaluation for auto accidents or other injury claims Return to Work Assessments: Determining work capacity and restrictions Treatment Appropriateness: Evaluating proposed medical treatments Maximum Medical Improvement: Determining if further treatment will provide benefit The IME Process: Medical Records Review: Thorough examination of all relevant medical records Comprehensive Physical Examination: Detailed assessment by Dr. Garcia Medical History Review: Discussion of symptoms, treatment history, and functional limitations Diagnostic Test Review: Analysis of lab work, imaging, and other test results Professional Report: Detailed written report with medical opinions and recommendations What's Included in the IME Report: Summary of medical records reviewed Patient history and chief complaints Findings from physical examination Review of diagnostic studies Medical opinions on diagnosis and causation Assessment of current functional status Work capacity and restrictions (if applicable) Treatment recommendations Prognosis and need for future care Dr. Garcia's Qualifications: As a board-certified Family Medicine physician, Dr. Garcia brings extensive experience in evaluating a wide range of medical conditions. Quick Turnaround: We understand that IME reports are often time-sensitive.
