gigiSafeHOUSE: 15 Years of Faithful Service

In 2010, I faced a personal health challenge that brought me to my knees. In that moment of desperation, I made a sacred covenant with God - if He would heal me, I would dedicate my work to serving others, removing barriers to healthcare access regardless of their ability to pay.

God was faithful. He healed me completely.

And I have kept my promise. For 15 years now, through gigiSafeHOUSE, I have worked tirelessly to ensure that healthcare is accessible to everyone - no matter their financial situation, insurance status, or life circumstances. This isn't just a nonprofit organization; it's my testimony of faithfulness to a promise made in my darkest hour.

Every person we help, every barrier we remove, every life we touch - it's all part of keeping that sacred covenant. gigiSafeHOUSE represents not just community service, but divine faithfulness and the joy of surprising people with compassionate care when they need it most.

Our mission: Providing affordable healthcare access for everyone without shame, combating obesity in families, friends, neighbors, and community - no matter the outcome, finding joy in the journey of service.

Learn more about our work and how you can support gigiSafeHOUSE at gigisafehouse.org

About Dr. Tess Garcia

Dr. Tess Garcia is a board-certified family medicine and obesity medicine physician dedicated to providing personalized, unhurried healthcare to the Blue Springs community and surrounding Kansas City metro area.

With Direct Primary Care, Dr. Garcia has created a practice model that prioritizes the doctor-patient relationship above all else - no insurance paperwork, no rushed appointments, just quality care focused on your whole health: Spirit, Body, and Soul.

Philosophy of Care

We believe in treating the whole patient - addressing not just physical symptoms, but the emotional and spiritual aspects of health as well. Our practice is built on compassion, evidence-based medicine, and the understanding that true wellness comes from caring for your complete self.
