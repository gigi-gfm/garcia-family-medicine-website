Welcome to Garcia Family Medicine

We believe in treating the whole patient - addressing not just physical symptoms, but the emotional and spiritual aspects of health as well. Our practice is built on compassion, evidence-based medicine, and the understanding that true wellness comes from caring for your complete self: Spirit, Body, and Soul.

Our Mission

Combating obesity in our families, friends, neighbors, and community - one person at a time, with compassion and evidence-based care. We provide Direct Primary Care that prioritizes the doctor-patient relationship above all else.
