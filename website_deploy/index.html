What is CoreLift with Emsella?

CoreLift is a breakthrough treatment using the BTL Emsella chair technology that delivers thousands of supramaximal pelvic floor muscle contractions in a single 28-minute session. It's like doing 11,000 Kegel exercises while you sit fully clothed in a comfortable chair.

Who Benefits from CoreLift?

This treatment is ideal for both men and women experiencing:

  • Urinary incontinence (stress, urge, or mixed)
  • Weak pelvic floor muscles
  • Intimate wellness concerns
  • Postpartum recovery needs
  • Age-related pelvic floor changes

What to Expect:

You remain fully clothed and simply sit in the Emsella chair. The treatment uses high-intensity focused electromagnetic (HIFEM) technology to stimulate deep pelvic floor muscles. Most patients describe a tingling sensation and muscle contractions. There's no pain, no downtime, and you can return to your normal activities immediately after treatment.

Treatment Protocol:

Most patients see optimal results with a series of 6 treatments, scheduled twice weekly over three weeks. Many patients notice improvement after just 2-3 sessions, with continued improvement over the following weeks.

Results:

Clinical studies show that 95% of patients report significant improvement in their quality of life. Many experience reduced pad usage, improved confidence, and enhanced intimate wellness.

What is an Independent Medical Evaluation?

An Independent Medical Evaluation (IME) is a comprehensive medical assessment performed by Dr. Tess Garcia for legal, insurance, or workers' compensation purposes. These evaluations provide objective, unbiased medical opinions to help resolve disputes or support legal proceedings.

When You Need an IME:

  • Workers' compensation claims
  • Personal injury cases
  • Disability determinations
  • Insurance claim disputes
  • Return-to-work assessments
  • Medical malpractice cases

What to Expect:

Your IME will include a thorough review of your medical records, a detailed medical history interview, a comprehensive physical examination, and any necessary diagnostic assessments. Dr. Tess will evaluate your current condition, causation, treatment received, and prognosis.

The IME Report:

You'll receive a detailed, professionally written report that includes medical history, examination findings, review of medical records, diagnostic impressions, causation analysis, treatment recommendations, and opinions on disability or impairment when applicable. Reports are typically completed within 2-3 weeks of the examination.

Why Choose Dr. Tess?

With over 25 years of family medicine experience, Dr. Tess provides thorough, objective evaluations with detailed documentation. Her reports are well-respected by attorneys, insurance companies, and courts. She understands the medical-legal process and provides clear, defensible opinions based on current medical evidence.

DOT Physical Examinations

Dr. Tess is a FMCSA-certified medical examiner qualified to perform Department of Transportation (DOT) physical examinations for commercial drivers. We understand that your time is valuable and your livelihood depends on maintaining your commercial driver's license.

Who Needs a DOT Physical?

  • Commercial truck drivers
  • Bus drivers
  • Drivers operating vehicles over 10,000 lbs
  • Drivers transporting hazardous materials
  • Drivers transporting 15+ passengers

What's Included:

Your DOT physical includes a comprehensive medical history review, vision and hearing testing, blood pressure and pulse checks, urinalysis, and a complete physical examination to ensure you meet FMCSA standards.

What to Bring:

  • Current driver's license
  • Glasses or contacts if you wear them
  • Hearing aid if you use one
  • List of current medications
  • Medical records if you have any chronic conditions

Certification Period:

Most drivers receive a 2-year certification. Drivers with certain medical conditions may receive shorter certification periods (3, 6, or 12 months) and will need monitoring and possible treatment to maintain their certification.

Same-Day Service:

We offer same-day DOT physicals with walk-in availability. Most exams are completed in 30-45 minutes, and you'll receive your Medical Examiner's Certificate immediately upon passing.
