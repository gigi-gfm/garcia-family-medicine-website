About Garcia Family Medicine

Dr. Tess Garcia

Board-Certified Family Medicine & Obesity Medicine Physician

Dr. Tess Garcia brings compassionate, comprehensive care to Blue Springs, Missouri. With board certifications in both Family Medicine and Obesity Medicine, Dr. Tess is uniquely qualified to address your whole-person health needs.

Our Direct Primary Care model means you get the time and attention you deserve. No rushed appointments, no insurance hassles - just quality healthcare focused on you.

We believe in treating the whole patient: Spirit, Body, and Soul. This holistic approach ensures that every aspect of your wellbeing is addressed.

Our Services

Welcome to Direct Primary Care

Discover how Direct Primary Care can transform your healthcare experience with affordable, accessible, and personalized medical care.

Direct Primary Care eliminates insurance hassles and provides you with direct access to your physician. Enjoy longer appointments, same-day or next-day visits, and comprehensive care tailored to your needs.

What is Direct Primary Care?

Learn about our innovative healthcare model that puts you first, without the complexity of traditional insurance.

With a simple monthly membership fee, you get unlimited access to primary care services, including office visits, phone consultations, basic lab work, and care coordination.

CoreLift with BTL Emsella

Revolutionary non-invasive treatment for pelvic floor strengthening and urinary incontinence.

CoreLift uses electromagnetic technology to strengthen pelvic floor muscles without surgery or downtime. Fully clothed treatments take just 28 minutes. Experience improved bladder control and confidence.

Weight Management & Obesity Medicine

Comprehensive, evidence-based weight management programs tailored to your unique needs.

Dr. Tess is board-certified in Obesity Medicine and offers personalized weight management programs including nutritional counseling, medication management, and lifestyle coaching to help you achieve sustainable results.
