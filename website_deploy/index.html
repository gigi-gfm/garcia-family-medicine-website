Independent Medical Examinations

Independent Medical Exams (IME)

Objective, thorough medical evaluations for legal, insurance, or disability cases. Professional assessments you can trust.

What is an IME?

An Independent Medical Exam is an objective medical evaluation performed by a qualified physician to assess an individual's health status, functional capacity, or disability level.

Common Uses:

  • Workers' compensation claims
  • Disability determinations
  • Personal injury cases
  • Insurance claim evaluations
  • Return-to-work assessments

Dr. Garcia provides thorough, unbiased evaluations with detailed written reports. Professional, respectful, and timely service.

DOT Physical Examinations

DOT Physicals

Department of Transportation physical exams for commercial drivers. Quick, thorough, and certified examinations to keep you on the road.

What is a DOT Physical?

A DOT physical is a medical examination required by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) for commercial motor vehicle drivers.

What to Expect:

  • Vision and hearing tests
  • Blood pressure and pulse check
  • Urinalysis
  • Physical examination
  • Review of medical history
  • Medical Examiner's Certificate issued on-site

Same-day appointments available. Walk-ins welcome. Get back on the road quickly!
