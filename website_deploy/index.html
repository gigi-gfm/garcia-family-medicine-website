Dr. Tess Garcia

Board Certified Family Medicine & Obesity Medicine

Hi, I'm Dr. Tess. I am Mexican American and Native American. I think my most important characteristic as a doctor is the fact that I listen, when so many others don't have the time or worse, don't take the time. I take tremendous pride in what I do, and I'm honored to serve our community with over 25 years of compassionate, comprehensive care.

Believe it or not, my undergraduate degree was in music education, and my first career was as an elementary school music teacher. I went to music school in New Mexico, where my parents are from, and later taught on the Blackfeet Reservation in northern Montana, then ended up in the DC metropolitan area until I moved to Richmond, Virginia, to attend the Medical College of Virginia.

I ended up in Missouri by matching for residency training at Truman Lakewood, formerly called Truman East. I graduated in 2002 and became a board-certified Family Physician. I worked for 5 years at the Samuel Rogers Community Health Center in Lexington, Missouri, then opened my own practicethe first woman doctor in Grain Valley in 2007and now in Blue Springs. I have basically spent my career being the "Family Doctor Near You." During this time, I also became a board-certified obesity medicine physician.

But that is just the geography of it all. How did I get from music teacher to family doctor? It was sudden when my mother called me on Halloween 1993 and asked me why I wasn't in medical school, but within a week, I was enrolled for the next semester taking prerequisite coursework. Operating at the same breakneck speed, I took the MCAT exams in 1994 and started medical school in 1995. Basically, my mother's question was asked at the right time in my life, and it suddenly made perfect sense.

I am a Sun Dancer, and I had never understood why I was called to be a dancer with what I was doing in my secular life. Then it dawned on methe Native American Sun Dance is a healing ceremony. At that realization, becoming a doctor just fit that spiritual context and seemed like the natural culmination of my life. And I realized at that time that my career was going to be dedicated to providing medical care to underserved people.

Now how I got to the dedication to caring for the underserved is kind of roundabout. My dad was in the Army, and that kept us moving when I was young, so really, I am from everywhere. I started public school in Germany and finished it in Alaska. My parents both came from humble Spanish families in northern New Mexico. My dad was the first member of his family to graduate from college. My mom was not the first on her side of the family, but she graduated too. Their careers after dad got out of the Army have been dedicated to helping the underserved. Even after they retired, through work for their church, they continued to serve the immigrant communities who attend that parish. They celebrated their 70th wedding anniversary before my father passed on last year at the age of 92. I was greatly influenced by them. You could say my desire to care for underserved patients was born in me. I was raised with it.

So, there it all is. Right here in Blue Springs, near to you, you have a family medicine physician, an obesity medicine specialist, a compassionate primary care physician, a board-certified family physician, a Mexican American physician, and a Native American physician, all at one convenient location. As a Direct Primary Care (DPC) physician, I am proud to offer a patient-first approach to medicineone that eliminates the barriers of insurance, provides affordable membership-based healthcare, and ensures my patients receive the time, attention, and personalized care they deserve. My mission is simple: to listen, to heal, and to serve.

Gigi

Practice Manager, Wellness Coach & gigiSafeHOUSE Founder

Hi, I'm Gigi. I'm a Filipina American author, nonprofit leader, and Practice Manager at Garcia Family Medicine. I founded gigiSafeHOUSE, a nonprofit organization that shares a synchronous relationship with our practiceraising funds to help people who can't afford quality healthcare.

What many people don't realize is that gigiSafeHOUSE and Garcia Family Medicine share a beautiful, synchronous relationship. Through gigiSafeHOUSE, we raise funds specifically to help people who can't afford quality healthcareensuring that financial barriers never stand between someone and the compassionate, comprehensive care they deserve. It's our way of living out Dr. Tess's mission to treat the whole patient: spirit, body, and soul.

In addition to my work here at the practice, I'm also an author. I wrote the children's book "Bahay Kubo" and I'm currently working on my second book. I describe myself as a shy introvert, but I've learned that quiet determination can move mountains. When I took on the IT responsibilities for our practice, I had zero technical experiencebut I rolled up my sleeves and taught myself GitHub, web development, and everything else we needed. That's how I approach everything: with persistence, heart, and a willingness to learn.

As Practice Manager and Wellness Coach, my role is to ensure that every aspect of your experience with us reflects the care and attention you deserve. From the moment you reach out to us, through every appointment and follow-up, I'm working behind the scenes to make sure your healthcare journey is seamless, supportive, and truly patient-centered.

Whether you're coming in for CoreLift, weight management, or comprehensive primary care, know that you're not just a patient to usyou're part of our community. And through the partnership between Garcia Family Medicine and gigiSafeHOUSE, we're committed to making sure that everyone in our community has access to the healthcare they need, regardless of their financial situation. I'm honored to serve alongside Dr. Tess and our incredible team, and I look forward to supporting you on your health journey.

Nourish & Thrive

A Comprehensive Component of Medical Weight Management

At Garcia Family Medicine, we believe true health goes beyond treating symptoms. Our Nourish & Thrive program is an integral part of our Medical Weight Management services, designed to help you achieve optimal wellness through personalized nutrition guidance, lifestyle coaching, and comprehensive support.

Personalized Nutrition Plans

Custom meal plans tailored to your health goals, preferences, and medical needs. Whether you're managing diabetes, heart health, or weight loss, we create a nutrition strategy that works for you.

Lifestyle Coaching

One-on-one coaching sessions to help you build sustainable healthy habits. We address sleep, stress management, exercise, and daily routines that support your wellness journey.

Progress Tracking

Regular check-ins and monitoring to track your progress. We celebrate your wins and adjust your plan as needed to ensure you're always moving forward.

Goal Setting & Support

Set realistic, achievable health goals with ongoing support from our team. You're never alone on your wellness journey - we're with you every step of the way.

Integrate mental wellness with physical health. Learn stress reduction techniques, mindfulness practices, and strategies to support emotional well-being.
