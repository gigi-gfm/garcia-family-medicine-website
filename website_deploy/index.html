CoreLift Pelvic Health

Revolutionary non-invasive electromagnetic therapy for pelvic floor strengthening and bladder control. FDA-cleared EMSELLA technology treats stress and urge incontinence in comfortable 28-minute sessions.

What is CoreLift?

CoreLift uses the FDA-cleared BTL EMSELLA chair, which delivers high-intensity focused electromagnetic (HIFEM) technology to strengthen your pelvic floor muscles. You remain fully clothed while sitting comfortably in the chair during your 28-minute treatment session.

How It Works:

The EMSELLA chair causes thousands of supramaximal pelvic floor muscle contractions in a single session‚Äîthe equivalent of doing thousands of Kegel exercises. These deep pelvic floor contractions restore neuromuscular control and strengthen the muscles that support your bladder and pelvic organs.

Conditions Treated:

  • Stress Incontinence: Leakage when coughing, sneezing, laughing, or exercising
  • Urge Incontinence: Sudden, strong urge to urinate with leakage
  • Pelvic Floor Weakness: Following childbirth or due to aging
  • Sexual Dysfunction: Improved pelvic muscle tone can enhance intimacy

Treatment Protocol:

  • Recommended series: 6 sessions over 3 weeks
  • Each session: 28 minutes
  • Fully clothed, non-invasive treatment
  • No downtime or recovery needed
  • Most patients see improvement after 3-4 sessions
  • Maintenance treatments every 6-12 months

Results You Can Expect:

Clinical studies show that 95% of patients report significant improvement in their quality of life. Many patients experience a dramatic reduction in pad usage, with some no longer needing pads at all. Results typically improve over 2-4 weeks following your treatment series.

Medical Weight Management

Achieving sustainable, long-term health through comprehensive physician-supervised weight management. This is not a quick fix‚Äîit's a commitment to lasting change.

Let Us Be Clear:

If you are looking for a 3-month program in which you starve yourself and use a GLP-1a to quickly take off pounds to get you into your bikini for the summer or fit into your wedding dress in time for the big day, our program is not for you. It takes time to change your eating habits so that once you come off the program, you will be able to maintain your weight loss. Our weight management program is for people who are committed to change.

What Is Our Weight Management Program?

Weight management at Garcia Family Medicine involves more than just losing pounds; it's about creating sustainable, healthy behaviors that reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, and hypertension and allow the patient to come off the program without experiencing weight regain.

The First Appointment:

  • Body composition analysis
  • Vital signs assessment
  • In-depth medical history
  • Thorough physical exam by Dr. Tess
  • Comprehensive lab panel to detect metabolic causes and comorbidities
  • Follow-up in 2 weeks to learn results and begin counseling

Program Components:

  • Biweekly Counseling with Dr. Tess: Understanding your relationship with food and emotional eating patterns
  • Biweekly Coaching with Gigi: Implementing changes and maintaining motivation
  • Nutrition Education: Learn what to eat and when‚Äînot about portion control or starvation
  • Exercise Program: Personalized to your fitness level and goals
  • Cyanocobalamin Injections: Fat-burning B12 injections to enhance metabolism
  • GLP-1a Agonists: Tirzepatide (with NAD precursors) when medically appropriate
  • 24/7 Support: Dr. Tess and Gigi available by text or phone to support you through challenges

About Tirzepatide:

Tirzepatide is more than just a GLP-1a agonist; it's also a GIP agonist. These hormones work in the GI system to make digestion slower, but also in the brain to decrease your appetite. Dr. Tess thoroughly evaluates each patient's eligibility before prescribing tirzepatide, ensuring that the treatment is safe, effective, and appropriate for their weight loss journey. You will not walk into the first appointment and walk out with tirzepatide‚Äîmedical evaluation comes first.

Why Choose Dr. Tess?

Dr. Tess is a board-certified Obesity Medicine Specialist who has proven the program's effectiveness to herself by losing 70 pounds‚Äîwithout Tirzepatide, but with the coaching help of Gigi who lost 70 pounds with her. Knowing ourselves how important this is, we hold your hand every step of the way.

We believe that any weight management program that perpetuates feelings of shame or failure should not be in business. Compassion and acceptance are major parts of our weight management program. We do not allow you to be alone on your weight loss journey.

Independent Medical Evaluations

Comprehensive, objective medical assessments for legal and administrative purposes. Dr. Garcia provides thorough IMEs for workers' comp, disability, and insurance cases.

What is an Independent Medical Evaluation (IME)?

An IME is a comprehensive medical examination conducted by an impartial physician to provide an objective assessment of a patient's medical condition, typically for legal, insurance, or workers' compensation purposes.

When You Need an IME:

  • Workers' compensation claims
  • Disability determinations
  • Personal injury litigation
  • Insurance claim disputes
  • Return-to-work assessments

What to Expect:

Dr. Garcia will conduct a thorough medical examination, review your medical records, and provide a detailed written report addressing specific questions about your condition, treatment, causation, and prognosis.

Professional, thorough, and objective evaluations. Schedule your IME today.

DOT Physicals

FMCSA-certified medical examinations for commercial drivers. Fast, professional service to keep you on the road.

What is a DOT Physical?

A DOT physical is a health examination mandated by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. Dr. Garcia is a certified medical examiner listed on the FMCSA National Registry.

The Examination Includes:

  • Vision and hearing tests
  • Blood pressure and pulse rate
  • Urinalysis
  • Physical examination
  • Review of medical history
  • Assessment of medications

What to Bring:

  • Current driver's license
  • List of current medications
  • Glasses or contacts (if you wear them)
  • Hearing aids (if you use them)
  • Medical records for any chronic conditions

Convenient Blue Springs Location:

Our office is conveniently located in Blue Springs, Missouri, easily accessible from I-70 and major trucking routes through the Kansas City metro area.

Get your DOT physical and get back on the road. Call (816) 427-5320 to schedule!
