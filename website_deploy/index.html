CoreLift Confidence with Emsella

Transform Your Pelvic Health

Revolutionary FDA-cleared treatment for pelvic floor strengthening...

CoreLift uses the BTL Emsella chair to deliver thousands of supramaximal pelvic floor contractions in just 28 minutes. This completely non-invasive treatment helps with:

  • Stress and urge incontinence
  • Pelvic floor weakness
  • Post-pregnancy pelvic floor recovery
  • Improved intimacy and confidence
  • No downtime - fully clothed treatment

Meet Our Team

Dr. Tess Garcia, DO

Board-Certified Family Medicine & Obesity Medicine Physician

Dr. Garcia is passionate about providing comprehensive, faith-based healthcare...

Dr. Tess Garcia graduated from Kansas City University of Medicine and Biosciences and completed her Family Medicine residency in Kansas City. She is board-certified in both Family Medicine and Obesity Medicine.

Dr. Garcia believes in treating the whole person‚ÄîSpirit, Body, and Soul. She combines evidence-based medicine with compassionate care, taking time to truly listen to her patients and address their individual needs.

Her special interests include:

  • Medical weight management and obesity medicine
  • Women's health and pelvic floor disorders
  • Mental health and wellness
  • Chronic disease management
  • Faith-based family medicine

When not caring for patients, Dr. Garcia enjoys spending time with her family and serving her community.

Gigi Garcia

Practice Manager & Wellness Coach

Gigi brings passion and personal experience to helping patients achieve their health goals...

Gigi is the heart of Garcia Family Medicine, managing practice operations while serving as a dedicated wellness coach. She founded gigiSafeHOUSE, a nonprofit organization focused on removing financial barriers to healthcare access.

As a Filipina American, Gigi brings cultural sensitivity and warmth to patient care. She is also a children's book author, having written "Bahay Kubo" to celebrate cultural heritage.

Gigi's approach to wellness coaching includes:

  • Personalized nutrition guidance
  • Lifestyle modification support
  • Accountability and encouragement
  • Resource navigation and assistance
  • Community health education

Her mission, rooted in a sacred covenant made in 2010, is to serve others with compassion "no matter the outcome."
